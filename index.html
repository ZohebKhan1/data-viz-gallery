About me

Bioinformatician working on investigating Hedgehog signaling-dependent gene regulatory networks governing cardiac, lung, esophageal, and neuronal development at the Moskowitz Lab at the University of Chicago. My work primarily involves time-series bulk RNA-seq, sc/snRNA-seq pseudotime analysis, and multi-omic studies with ATAC-seq/ChIP-seq and Hi-C/Micro-C to define enhancer–target gene interactions.

My previous research investigated Tbx5-dependent gene regulatory networks in atrial fibrillation pathogenesis through integrative analysis of GWAS data, linkage disequilibrium mapping, and ENCODE consortium epigenomic datasets to identify regulatory variants that modulate transcription factor binding sites.

About this gallery

The purpose of this gallery to showcase various data visualizations methods that I have generated over the past year, primarily for presenting time series bulk RNAseq or ATAC seq data. While I primarily used these visualization methods for genomic analyses, the majority of these figures can quite easily be repurposed depending on the modality of your data.
