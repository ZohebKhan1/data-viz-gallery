About me

Bioinformatician working on investigating Hedgehog signaling-dependent gene regulatory networks governing cardiac, lung, esophageal, and neuronal development at the Moskowitz Lab at the University of Chicago. B.A. Biology w/ Genetics Specialization @ The University of Chicago, 2022. My work primarily is involved with time series bulk RNA seq, sc/snRNAseq psuedotime analysis, multi-omic studies with ATAC/ChIPseq and Hi-C/Micro-C to define enhancer-target gene interactions.

My previous research investigated Tbx5-dependent gene regulatory networks in atrial fibrillation pathogenesis through integrative analysis of GWAS data, linkage disequilibrium mapping, and ENCODE consortium epigenomic datasets to identify regulatory variants that modulate transcription factor binding sites.
